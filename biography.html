Lee Ufan
is as an
"artist-philosopher"
born in southern Korea in 1936.
"To philosophize is to create concepts,"
said Gilles Deleuze.

Logically, one can therefore say that Lee Ufan's work is widely
"conceptual"
. Specifically, it is part of the
Mono-ha
movement or
"school things"
, an artistic movement of the late 60s / begin 70s born in Japan (Lee Ufan lived and worked mainly in Korea, Japan and France). One might compare this movement of the Italian
Arte Povera
movement or
post-minimalism in the United States.
